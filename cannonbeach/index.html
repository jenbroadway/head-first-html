Cannon Beach birthday trip

February 18th, 2011

When we got to Cannon Beach it had snowed the night before. It was very cold, but the sun was out and it was warm enough to take off our coats by 2pm. Even though it's only Feb. 18th, the bulbs and camelias are in bloom.

The sunset was beautiful. The first picture is of the creek that outflows onto the beach. The second is the view from a path through some bushes that I found behind one of the hotels.

February 19th,2011

A day on the beach

Walking back from the beach, we saw a dreadlocked poodle and a very nice piece of metalwork.

February 20th,2011

For my birthday dinner we went to Manzanita and had dinner at Left Coast Siesta with my friend Sarah and her girls, Susanna and Marcella. I had a green chile enchilada. It was made with spinach tortillas, chicken, green chile sauce, sour cream, cheese, beans, and olives. Then, Sarah gave me a dark chocolate/ginger candy bar. Yum!
